Founder & CEO of LivUnity

The idea of LivUnity came to me while I was searching for a certified personal trainer to help me heal my back pain and get back to running, something I had become deeply passionate about.
With my background in technology, fitness, and business, I decided to build an innovative solution to booking appointments with fitness experts.
LivUnity makes it easy for you to increase your physical activity, by getting you to the coach who will guide you to reach your fitness goals of looking confident, feeling stronger, losing weight or living better.
Today, LivUnity is the only fitness services marketplace connecting expert fitness trainers and advice-seeking individuals.
